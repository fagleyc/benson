Pair a Phone
  1. On the phone whose WhatsApp account Benson should use: open WhatsApp → Settings → Linked devices → Link a device.
  2. Scan the QR code below.
  3. Once paired, this card disappears and the status above turns green.

⚠ This pairs Benson with whatever WhatsApp account scans the QR. Use a number you're OK Benson reading and writing on. Family can DM that number or add it to a household group.

Allowlist

Who can talk to Benson via WhatsApp. Edit /etc/benson/env and restart benson: 

WHATSAPP_ALLOWED_NUMBERS=15551112222,15553334444@s.whatsapp.net,1234567890123@g.us
WHATSAPP_SPEAKER_MAP=15551112222:Casey,15553334444:Lindsey

Numbers without @ are treated as direct-message JIDs. Group JIDs end in @g.us. Anyone not on the allowlist is silently ignored.
